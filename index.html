RPA Development of a Vendor Onboarding Solution

The goal of this project is to streamline and optimize the vendor onboarding process at a Financial Institution by implementing Robotic Process Automation (RPA). The existing manual process involves several time-consuming tasks, including the handling of vendor registration emails, validation of tax IDs, and generation of management information system (MIS) reports. These tasks are currently performed by a subject matter expert (SME), leading to inefficiencies such as errors, delays, and resource overuse. The Key Objectives were to increase efficiency by enabling existing staff to manage a 50% increase in new vendors by automating repetitive tasks. To decrease the time taken to onboard new vendors by 80%, ensuring a faster and more reliable process, and to improve accuracy by ensure all activity logs and reports are generated daily, reducing the manual follow-up due to incomplete or incorrect information.

Canteen Ordering System Initiative

As part of my capstone project, I analyzed and provided business solutions for an FMCG canteen ordering system, focusing on streamlining operations and enhancing employee satisfaction. The current "As-Is" system suffers from inefficiencies such as long waiting times, overcrowding, and food waste, which result in reduced employee productivity and increased costs. In my "To-Be" solution, I recommended the implementation of a web-based ordering platform, integrated with payroll for automated payments, and a delivery handling system to improve meal availability and reduce wait times.
